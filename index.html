Fast and easy to use

Swipe the plant to the right for watering and swipe left for fertilizing. You can search plants quickly by their name using the search feature.

The first two plant actions are assigned to the left and right swipes. By default, the Watering and Fertilizing are assigned to the left and right swipes as they are the most used actions for plants. Do you have a lot of plants to care? Worry not, you can use the search feature to search your plants by name. Just tap on the search button at the top and enter name of the plant. Entering the first character of the plant name will display all the plants whose name starts with that particular character. If you enter multiple characters, the app will display the plants that have those characters anywhere in their names. This is useful when you don't remember exact name of the plant, just type any word that you remember and the app will take care of the rest.

Unlimited plant actions

Growlog is not limited to just Watering and Fertilizing. You can add as many plant actions as you would like. Each plant also has its own section for writing notes.

To mark an action as done, just tap the checkmark button of the action and the Growlog will take care of logging the history as well as diplaying when the action was last done. You can add actions such as Sterilizing, Leaf Cleaning, Pruning, Full Draining, Harvesting, Repotting, Air layering and so on. Be default, Growlog adds Watering and Fertilizing for every new plant that you add. After that you can add any actions as feel necessary for that particular plant. Every plant has it's own list of actions. The note feature allows you to write miscellenious things about the plant such as the kind of soil mix used, the concentration of the fertilizer used or write any experience about that particular plant. Use the note icon at the top right corner to write the notes.

All reminders in one place

You can set repeating reminders for plant actions and get a single daily notification for all the reminders. You also have the ability to postpone a reminder.

The reminders screen displays all the plant actions that need your attention. You get a daily notification if there are any actions required by you. To set the reminder for a plant action, tap the alarm icon for that particular action and specify the repeating number of days after which to remind you of that action. The reminder is triggered when the specified number of days exceeds the number of days since the plant action was last done. You can also postpone a reminder when you think it's not an appropriate time to perform that action. Use the snooze icon to specify the number of days by which the reminder should be postponed.
